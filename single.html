Trip vines is a travel website sharing details through video for the travel lovers.

Challenges

Sites goal is to learn about the destinations through video. Similar to lonely planet but in video form. Destination page separately for each where users can comment on posts. Target was to finish the site in 2pages.

What I did

Going through the process there was challenges that only video will not help to understand users about what they are going to watch. I come up with a simple solution in homepage to show the destinations with country and a title which will help the user to understand more clearly. 2 pages was not enough for the site so I offered a destination page were all the destinations will show separately rather than all in homepage. Lastly the destination page were all locations will show the details of each. I came up with an idea of dropdown were users can select the locations in the same page.
